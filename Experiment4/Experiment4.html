Experiment 4

Contact Form Designs using HTML Form Elements

Theory

  1. What are the main form elements in HTML and their purposes?
  2. How do radio buttons and checkboxes differ in user interaction?
  3. Explain the use of <select> and <option> tags in forms.
  4. What is the difference between the submit and reset buttons in a form?
